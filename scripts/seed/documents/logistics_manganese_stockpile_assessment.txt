- TOTAL (all grades): 48,000 MT

DEMAND COVERAGE ANALYSIS:
Current monthly battery-grade manganese consumption: 640 MT (based on Q4 FY2024 actuals)
High-purity MnSO4 stockpile coverage: 19,400 / 640 = 30.3 months (STRONG)
FY2026 projected monthly demand: 1,800 MT
Coverage at FY2026 demand: 19,400 / 1,800 = 10.8 months (ADEQUATE — monitor closely)
FY2028 projected monthly demand: 4,300 MT
Coverage at FY2028 demand: 19,400 / 4,300 = 4.5 months (CRITICAL — below 6-month target)

RECOMMENDATIONS:
1. Increase MnSO4 procurement by 8,000 MT from South Africa (Kalahari Manganese) — Q2 FY26
2. Evaluate MOIL (Manganese Ore India Limited) integration to produce battery-grade MnSO4 
   from domestic ore (Balaghat and Dongri Buzurg mines, Maharashtra)
3. Develop NCMM-MOIL joint processing MOU for INR 380 crore capital investment

DOMESTIC SUPPLY INTEGRATION:
MOIL Limited, Nagpur (India's largest manganese miner) produces 1.5 Mt/year of 
manganese ore from Nagpur and Bhandara districts, Maharashtra. NCMM assessment 
indicates 18% of current MOIL output could be upgraded to MnSO4 battery grade with 
INR 380 crore processing plant investment.

SUPPLY CHAIN RISK:
Battery-grade MnSO4 processing globally dominated by China (95%). South Africa 
(Kalahari Manganese Fields) provides ore, but processing is performed in China. 
India needs domestic MnSO4 processing capability — identified as strategic gap.
