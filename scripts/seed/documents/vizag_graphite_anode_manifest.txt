SECTION 10: STRATEGIC SIGNIFICANCE NOTE

This consignment of 1,500 MT synthetic graphite anode material is one of the first large-scale procurements 
under the PLI Scheme for Advanced Chemistry Cell (ACC) Battery Storage (Tranche-2). It supports the NCMM 
graphite supply mapping initiative. India currently imports 100% of its battery-grade synthetic graphite 
from Japan (Mitsubishi Chemical, Showa Denko) and China. This consignment partially de-risks India's near-
term dependency on Chinese graphite suppliers. NCMM Technology Cell to report on this consignment's role 
in the domestic anode material production roadmap (target: 40% domestic sourcing by FY2028-29).
