FY2025-26 PROCUREMENT TARGETS BY MINERAL:

1. LITHIUM (Target: 25,000 MT LCE strategic reserve by March 2026)
   - Q1 FY26: 6,000 MT from Australia (Pilbara, Liontown Resources)
   - Q2 FY26: 5,500 MT from Argentina (Livent Corporation)
   - Q3 FY26: 7,000 MT from Chile (SQM, Codelco tie-up)
   - Q4 FY26: 6,500 MT from Australia + spot market
   - Budget: INR 3,200 crore

2. COBALT (Target: 12,000 MT Co reserve by March 2026)
   - Q1 FY26: 2,000 MT from DRC (Glencore)
   - Q2 FY26: 1,800 MT from Philippines (Nickel Asia by-product)
   - Q3 FY26: 2,200 MT from DRC (diversified suppliers)
   - Q4 FY26: 2,000 MT from Australia (Cobalt Blue Holdings, if operational)
   - Budget: INR 2,800 crore

3. NICKEL (Target: 60,000 MT Class I Ni reserve by March 2026)
   - Quarterly procurement: 3,750 MT × 4 = 15,000 MT
   - Primary: Philippines (Nickel Asia Corp), Indonesia (through non-Chinese smelters)
   - Budget: INR 4,100 crore

4. MANGANESE (Target: 35,000 MT high-purity reserve by March 2026)
   - Domestic integration: 5,000 MT from MOIL (Odisha/Maharashtra operations)
   - Import: 2,000 MT from South Africa (Kalahari), Gabon (Comilog)
   - Budget: INR 800 crore (lower due to domestic sourcing)

5. GRAPHITE (Target: 8,000 MT spherical graphite reserve by March 2026)
   - Japan (Mitsubishi Chemical, Tokai Carbon): 3,500 MT
   - Mozambique (Syrah Resources): 2,500 MT
   - Domestic (Arunachal Pradesh deposit feasibility): 2,000 MT (subject to clearances)
   - Budget: INR 1,200 crore

6. RARE EARTH ELEMENTS (Target: 3,500 MT REO reserve by March 2026)
   - Australia (Lynas Rare Earths): 1,500 MT mixed REO
   - Canada (Mkango Resources): 800 MT NdPr oxide
   - Brazil (CBMM, Sigma Lithium): 1,200 MT
   - Budget: INR 2,400 crore

TOTAL FY2025-26 PROCUREMENT BUDGET: INR 18,500 CRORE

BILATERAL AGREEMENTS SIGNED (FY2024-25, effective FY26):
1. India-Australia Critical Minerals Investment Partnership (CMIP) — AUD 3.2B framework
2. India-Argentina Lithium MoU — 20-year supply agreement, INR 8,200 crore
3. India-Chile Technology Partnership — SQM technical collaboration
4. India-Canada Critical Minerals Strategy 2025 — 15-year framework
5. India-Japan Battery Technology MoU — Graphite supply + cathode technology
6. India-USA Minerals Security Partnership (MSP) — Multilateral framework
7. India-Tanzania Graphite MoU — Mahenge deposit, 10-year offtake

STRATEGIC RESERVE TARGETS (End FY2025-26):
- Lithium Carbonate: 25,000 MT (24-month demand coverage)
- Cobalt (Co content): 12,000 MT (36-month coverage target)
- Class I Nickel: 60,000 MT (18-month coverage)
- High-purity Manganese: 35,000 MT (24-month coverage)
- Spherical Graphite: 8,000 MT (12-month coverage)
- Mixed REO: 3,500 MT (18-month coverage)

DOMESTIC PRODUCTION MILESTONES (FY2026-27):
- Rajasthan Lithium (Degana-Nagaur): Feasibility study completion Q2 FY26
- Jharkhand Cobalt (Singhbhum belt): Resource estimation Q3 FY26
- Karnataka Nickel (Chitradurga): Environmental clearance pending
